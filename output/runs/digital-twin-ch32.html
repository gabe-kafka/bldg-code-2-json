32.4.1 Sign
text_block Convention The combined effects of internal pressures and atmospheric pressure change, expressed in coef fi cient form by ( GC piT ), shall follow the same sign convention as provided in Section 26.4, where positive pressure acts toward the surface and negative pressure acts away from the surface.

32.4.2 Critical Load Condition
text_block Values of external pressures shall be combined algebraically with the combined effects of internal pressures and atmospheric pressure change to determine the most critical load.
