11.1.2 Scope
text_block Every structure and portion thereof, including nonstructural components, shall be designed and constructed to resist the effects of earthquake motions as prescribed by the seismic requirements of this standard. Certain nonbuilding structures, as described in Chapter 15, are also within the scope and shall be designed and constructed in accordance with the requirements of Chapter 15. The following
text_block Detached oneand two-family dwellings that are located where the mapped, short period, spectral response acceleration parameter, SS , is less than 0.4 or where the seismic design category determined in accordance with Section 11.6 is A, B, or C.
→ ASCE7-22-11.6-H1
text_block Detached oneand two-family wood-frame dwellings not included in Exemption 1 with not more than two stories above grade plane, satisfying the limitations of and constructed in accordance with the IRC.
text_block Agricultural storage structures that are intended only for incidental human occupancy.
text_block Nonbuilding structures that require special consideration of their response characteristics and environment that are not addressed in Chapter 15 and for which other regulations provide seismic criteria, such as vehicular bridges, electrical transmission towers, hydraulic structures, buried utility lines and their appurtenances, and nuclear reactors.
text_block Piers and wharves that are not accessible to the general public.
11.1.3 Applicability
text_block Structures and their nonstructural components shall be designed and constructed in accordance with the requirements of the following chapters based on the type of structure or component: Buildings: Chapter 12
text_block Nonbuilding structures: Chapter 15
text_block Nonstructural components: Chapter 13
text_block Seismically isolated structures: Chapter 17
text_block Structures with damping systems: Chapter 18
11.1.4 Alternate Materials and Methods of Construction
text_block Alternate materials and methods of construction to those prescribed in the seismic requirements of this standard shall not be used unless approved by the Authority Having Jurisdiction. Substantiating evidence shall be submitted demonstrating that the proposed alternate will be at least equal in strength, durability, and seismic resistance for the purpose intended.
11.1.5 Quality Assurance
text_block Quality assurance for seismic forceresisting systems and other designated seismic systems de fi ned in Section 13.2.2 shall be provided in accordance with the requirements of the Authority Having Jurisdiction.
